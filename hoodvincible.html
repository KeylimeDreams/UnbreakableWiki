Hoodvincible

Backstory

Hoodvincible began his life far from heroism. Born into a violent criminal empire, he rose through the ranks of the mafia with a mix of cold intelligence and overwhelming physical talent. By his early twenties, he had already become one of the most feared underworld leaders on the planet — a strategist who preferred precision over chaos, and power over mercy.

His life changed when he discovered the truth about Omni-Man’s presence on Earth. Unlike most, Hoodvincible didn’t fear the Viltrumite — he saw him as a threat to his empire, a destabilizing force that could not be controlled. Instead of facing Omni-Man directly, he made a move no one expected: he summoned Battle Beast.

Through a combination of negotiation, tribute, and manipulation, Hoodvincible convinced Battle Beast to confront Omni-Man. The resulting clash was catastrophic. Omni-Man fell — not to a hero, but to a criminal mastermind who understood how to weaponize the universe’s most dangerous warrior.

But victory came with a price. Angstrom Levy, monitoring multiversal disruptions, detected the anomaly caused by Omni-Man’s premature death. Seeing potential in Hoodvincible’s ruthlessness, Angstrom forcibly summoned him into the chaos of the Invincible War, pulling him across realities to serve as a wildcard in the multiversal conflict.

Now displaced from his home universe, Hoodvincible operates with a single goal: rebuild his empire on a cosmic scale — no matter which version of reality he must conquer to do it.
