Characters

Hoodvincible

A ruthless mafia-born tactician who orchestrated Omni-Man’s downfall by manipulating Battle Beast into the fight. After being abducted by Angstrom Levy during the Invincible War, he now seeks to rebuild his criminal empire across the multiverse.

Omnivincible

A What‑If version of Mark Grayson who accepted Omni-Man’s mission and took his mantle. Believing he could “do it better,” he unified Earth through controlled force and later entered the multiverse to impose his doctrine of absolute responsibility.

Lightblueincible

A calm, emotionally muted variant of Mark whose Viltrumite mutation removed fear and rage. A perfect strategist with no wasted motion, he entered the Invincible War voluntarily to stabilize the multiverse through logic alone.
