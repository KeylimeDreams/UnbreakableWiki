The Unbreakable Project

The Unbreakable Wiki chronicles a fan series set in a reimagined corner of the Invincible Universe. Here you’ll find original characters, alternate timelines, and new narrative arcs that explore what happens when familiar events fracture, diverge, or break entirely.

Created and written by LazarusXeo. This wiki documents the evolving canon of the Unbreakable continuity: character bios, power sets, affiliations, and key storylines.
