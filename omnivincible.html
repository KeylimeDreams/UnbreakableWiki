Omnivincible

Backstory

In this universe, Mark Grayson didn’t reject Omni-Man’s mission — he inherited it. After Nolan fled Earth, Mark was left with a world terrified of Viltrumites and on the brink of collapse. Instead of resisting the legacy, he embraced it.

He became Omnivincible, Earth’s self-appointed overseer. Unlike Nolan, he didn’t rule through terror. He ruled through strict intervention, global oversight, and an unbreakable doctrine: “Peace through responsibility.”

He dismantled wars before they began, neutralized threats before they escalated, and forced nations into cooperation. To some, he was a savior. To others, a tyrant.

When the Viltrum Empire returned, they found Earth already unified — under Mark’s command. His defiance fractured the Empire, earning him enemies and admirers.

Angstrom Levy abducted him during the Invincible War, expecting a weapon. Instead, Omnivincible saw a multiverse in need of order. Now he travels reality enforcing his doctrine, believing only he can prevent universal collapse.
